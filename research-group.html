Alumni

  • Dr Swarna Bindu Chetty

    Research Associate, University of York, UK  ·  PhD 2019–2023

    Named among the Top 100 Women in 6G globally (2025), Swarna's doctoral research focused on AI-driven next-generation communications. She is currently developing AI-powered Radio Access Network (RAN) intelligent controllers for 5G telecom infrastructure on the UK government-funded YO-RAN project.

  • Dr Guoxin Wang

    Research Associate, Trinity College Dublin  ·  PhD 2019–2024

    Recruited through UCD's MoU with the China Scholarship Council, Guoxin's research focused on self- and unsupervised learning for physiological time-series (ECG), wearable sensing and edge AI, and efficient model deployment via quantisation and pruning.

  • Dr Mouli Chakraborty

    Research Associate, Queen's University Belfast  ·  PhD 2020–2025

    Mouli's research spans mathematical aspects of quantum communication, quantum information processing, quantum computing, machine learning, satellite-based quantum communication, and quantum machine learning. Her work was recognised with the IEEE Antennas and Propagation Society Graduate Fellowship — Quantum Technologies Initiative 2021.
